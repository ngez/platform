Lazy Renderer

Html and components are rendered until they've actually entered the viewport.

Features

  • Lazy load images.
  • Components are instantiated until they've entered the viewport, thus delaying their lifecycle hooks.

Demo

Open your devtools and scroll down. Notice how the component's ngOnInit method is called until it enters the viewport. Go to the network tab and the image was also lazily loaded.

Import

Begin by importing the module like this: 

    import {{'{'}} NgEzLazyRendererModule {{'}'}} from '@ngez/core';
